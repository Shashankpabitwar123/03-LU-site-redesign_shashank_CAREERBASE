About Fran

A life rooted in flowers and community

Meet Francine Miller

Raised on weekend trips to the Ferry Plaza Farmers Market, Fran learned early that flowers connect people. In 1995 she opened Fran’s Flowers on Valencia Street with a simple promise: make it easy to share care and joy, every day.

Today she mentors local apprentices, sources from Bay Area growers whenever possible, and dedicates a portion of every purchase to organizations addressing homelessness. If you catch her humming in the shop, she’s probably designing a sunflower bundle for someone’s birthday.
